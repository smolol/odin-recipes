Yummy Hamborg

Secret Hamborg recipe

- Hamborg is layered

- To make yummy Hamborg, mmmmmh

Ingredients

  • Bunz
  • Ground Bef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomate
  • Choese
  • Mutard
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef > Make balls > squish balls > cook until not pink.
  2. Toast Bunz.
  3. Small Bun > Mutard/Mayo > Beef > Choese > Lettuce > Tomate.
  4. Yummy Hamborg!
